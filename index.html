Inoui is a multidisciplinary design collective specialising in art direction, installation and digital technologies within the realms of fashion, beauty, art, luxury and lifestyle.

Working on projects which bring together different creative disciplines is what we enjoy most.

Over the years we have developed a variety of creative and commercial projects, with an emphasis on emerging technologies, interactivity and new media as well as a more traditional hands-on approach with illustration, photography and product design.

The above-mentioned installation works are the continuation and the merging of the skills of each member of our team. Our experience ranges from illustration, set design, photography, interior design to video and programming.
